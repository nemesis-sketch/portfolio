Detailed Biography

I'm Seth Dison C. Sepe — an undergraduate student pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Systems at Davao del Norte State College (DNSC). My academic journey has been shaped by a deep curiosity for how technology can serve people, organizations, and communities in meaningful ways.

Beyond the classroom, I find expression in creative pursuits: photography that captures unscripted moments, video editing that tells visual stories, and graphic design that communicates with clarity and elegance. I believe that the best technology solutions are those that marry function with beauty — and that belief drives everything I build.

I am passionate about continuous learning, purposeful leadership, and using my skills in service of causes that matter. I am actively seeking opportunities to grow professionally and to contribute to projects where creativity and technology converge.

Educational Background

I am currently in my third year at Davao del Norte State College, majoring in Information Systems. My coursework has given me a solid foundation in systems analysis, database management, web technologies, programming, and project management.

My academic interests extend into human-computer interaction and the practical application of IS theory to real-world organizational needs — reflected in my published research paper in the International Journal of Scientific Research and Engineering Development (IJSRED, Vol. 8, Issue 3), titled "Information System Technology Plan for Net Central Game Central Panabo."

Leadership & Organizational Experience

As the Executive Secretary of the Institute of Computing Local Student Government (IC-LSG), I manage official correspondence, prepare reports and documentation, coordinate events, and support the organization's day-to-day administrative operations. Notable events I've contributed to include BYTEFEST 3.0 and the PSITS Region 11 Festival of Talents 2026.

My leadership style emphasizes clarity, attention to detail, and genuine care for the people I work with. I believe that great organizations are built on communication, accountability, and a shared sense of purpose.

Career Goals

My immediate goal is to graduate with distinction and step into a role where I can apply my skills in information systems, creative design, and project coordination at a meaningful scale. I aim to work in environments that value both technical rigor and human-centered design.

Long-term, I aspire to build tools and experiences that empower communities — whether through thoughtfully designed information systems, compelling visual content, or advocacy work that drives real change.
